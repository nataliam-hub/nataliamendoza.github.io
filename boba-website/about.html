All About Boba

Description of Boba

Boba, or otherwise known as bubble tea, is a black jelly in
shape of a ball put into tea drinks. It originated in Taiwan
and has continued to become more known. Boba comes in tea and is
paired with tapioca pearls. The pearls have a soft and chewy
texture.
